 本模板为极简风格单栏word简历模板，适用于升学申请。包含中文和英文两个版本。
 
 This is a clear and simple single-column word format resume template suitable for postgraduate applications, including two versions (Chinese and English).

 ## Core Principle
世界上并不存在所谓的“完美”简历模板，无需在挑选模板上焦虑！关键在于如何有效利用模板，尽可能丰富内容，让审阅者能够轻松、顺畅地阅读。

There is not really a right way to write a resume, but you can make the viewing easier for reviewers.

## Postgraduate Application Template

建议将简历控制在一页以内，除非你确实有足够丰富的内容需要展示。如果必须超过一页，也请尽量将第二页写满，避免出现只有半页的情况。我会用先后顺序介绍模板中的内容。

It is recommended to keep your resume to one page, unless you have substantial content that requires more space. If you do need a second page, try to fill it completely rather than leaving it half-empty. I will introduce the contents of the template in sequential order.

### Personal Information

个人信息部分通常包括你的全名、联系电话、个人邮箱以及升学目标。你也可以附上个人主页，不过导师们未必有时间逐一查看。

The personal information section typically includes your full name, contact number, personal email address, and objective. You may also include a link to your personal website, though advisors may not have time to review.

请尽量使用你的学校邮箱作为你的个人邮箱。

Please use your school email as your personal email address.

升学目标（可选）：用一句话说明你的当前的状态以及你希望从事什么研究。例如：应届本科生，寻求攻读医学人工智能方向的博士学位的机会。

Objective (Optional): Use one simple sentence to describe your current status and the research you wish to pursue. For example: An undergraduate student seeking a PhD position in Medical AI.

我个人通常不建议在简历中添加个人照片。从升学申请的角度来看，照片并没有实质性的参考价值，而且如果导师对你感兴趣，后续交流时自然也能看到你的样子。

I generally do not recommend including a personal photo in this section. From the perspective of graduate school applications, it does not carry significant value. Besides, if advisors are interested, they will naturally see you during later interviews.

### Education

按照时间倒序列出你的教育经历，如有交换经历，可放在该部分的最后。

List your educational experiences in reverse chronological order. If you have exchange experiences, place them at the end of this section.

每段教育经历需包含以下信息：学校、专业、起止时间、GPA、排名及相关课程。 在选择相关课程时，建议按以下优先级排序：1、申请方向强相关 + 分数高；2、申请方向弱相关 + 分数高；3、申请方向强相关 + 分数低。

For each educational experience, include: school, major, start and end dates, GPA, rank, and relevant courses. When listing relevant courses, prioritize in this order: 1. High relevance + High grade; 2. Low relevance + High grade; 3. High relevance + Low grade.

 如果你有国家奖学金，可以写在这里。

 If you have received the National Scholarship, you may list it here. Other honors need not be included.
